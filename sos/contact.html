Directions

From Route 2:

Take Exit 14 (West River Street, Lake Mattawa). Turn South off exit ramp (from the west, turn right, from the east, turn left) onto Holtshire Road. Follow this road approximately 1.5 miles to the first intersection at which point you will see, but not pass a large lake (Mattawa). Turn right onto Chestnut Hill Road. Proceed uphill 1/2 mile to the ” T “. Turn left ( still on Chestnut Hill Rd.). In approx. 1/2 mile the road forks–bear right (still Chestnut Hill –do not take the left fork which is Lower Chestnut Hill!). Continue 3/4 mile. We have two parking areas: one on the left near some strings of colorful prayer flags, or, continue to our farmstand on the left, then turn into our driveway and immediately left to pull into the parking area. Please note that the house, barn, and parking across the street is our neighbor, not us! Unless you have limited mobility, please park and walk down the driveway if coming for a workshop.
